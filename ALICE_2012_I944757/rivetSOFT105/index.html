Measurement of charm production at central rapidity in proton-proton collisions at $\sqrt{s}=7$ TeV (ALICE_2012_I944757)

Inspire | HepData | DOI:10.1007/JHEP01(2012)128 | arXiv:1111.1553

CERN-LHC. Measurement of the differential production cross sections of prompt $p_T$ charmed mesons, D$^0$, D$^{+-}$ and D*$^{+-}$ as a function of $p_T$, produced in proton-proton collisions at a centre-of-mass energy of 7 TeV. The data, collected during 2010 with a minimum-bias trigger, have a total integrated luminosity of 5nb$^{-1}$ and cover the $p_T$ range 1-24 GeV with absolute rapidity < 0.5.
