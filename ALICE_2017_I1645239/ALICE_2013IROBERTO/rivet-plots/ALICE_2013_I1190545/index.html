Measuremements of the charged-particle pseudorapidity density over 4 units of pseusdorapidity in non-single diffractive (NSD) proton-Lead collisions at a centre-of-mass energy per nucleon pair of 5.02 TeV. The average value at midrapidity is measured to be 16.81 $\pm$ 0.71(sys) which corresponds to 2.14 $\pm$ 0.17(sys) per participating nucleon.
